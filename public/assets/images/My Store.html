About Us

Hybrid Beauty and Aesthetics Training is South Africas premier beauty academy dedicated to providing comprehensive, high-quality training in beauty and aesthetics.

Located in Johannesburg, our academy is a haven for aspiring beauty professionals who seek to excel in their craft and establish themselves as industry leaders.

Academy Core Values

Compassionate | Professional | Disciplined

Excellence: Commitment to delivering superior education and fostering a standard of perfection in every service offered.
Empowerment: Encouraging personal growth and self-confidence to ensure each student achieves their career aspirations.
Innovation: Staying ahead of industry trends by incorporating modern techniques and tools into our curriculum.
Integrity: Building trust through professionalism, ethical practices, and high standards.
Inclusivity: Welcoming students from diverse backgrounds and supporting their individual journeys.

Programs and Training

At Hybrid Beauty and Aesthetics Training, we offer a comprehensive range of courses designed to cater to every aspect of the beauty industry, with added mentorship and short business training for each program. Our student fees include essential starter kits tailored to your training program. These kits are yours to keep, ensuring you have the tools needed to either start your own business or refine your skills through practice after training. Additionally, we provide a professional uniform for you to wear throughout the duration of the course, fostering a polished and industry-ready image.
